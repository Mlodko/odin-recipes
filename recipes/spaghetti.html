Spaghetti

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti pasta
  • Olive oil
  • Tomato sauce
  • Garlic
  • Basil
  • Oregano
  • Salt and pepper
  • Onion
  • Ground beef
  • Red wine

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil.
  2. Cook spaghetti according to package instructions until al dente.
  3. Meanwhile, heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
  4. Add diced onion and garlic and saut√© until softened.
  5. Add ground beef and cook until browned and no longer pink.
  6. Add tomato sauce, red wine, salt, and black pepper to the skillet and bring to a simmer.
  7. Simmer for 10-15 minutes until sauce has thickened.
  8. Toss spaghetti with the sauce and serve immediately.
