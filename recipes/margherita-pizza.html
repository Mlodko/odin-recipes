Margherita pizza

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Basil leaves
  • Salt
  • Olive oil

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 450°F (230°C).
  2. Roll out pizza dough on a floured surface to a 12-inch (30cm) circle.
  3. Transfer the dough to a pizza stone or baking sheet.
  4. Spread tomato sauce over the dough, leaving a 1-inch (2.5cm) border.
  5. Add fresh mozzarella cheese on top of the sauce.
  6. Top with fresh basil leaves and a pinch of salt.
  7. Bake for 10-12 minutes until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is melted.
  8. Remove from oven, slice, and serve hot.
